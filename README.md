**Zomato Restaurant Data Analysis**

**Business Intelligence & Market Insights Report**

**Executive Summary :**
This project presents a comprehensive data analysis of Zomato restaurant data to identify key market trends, customer behavior patterns, and strategic business opportunities within the food delivery ecosystem.
Using Python-based data analytics techniques, the project transforms raw restaurant data into actionable insights that support data-driven decision-making for business growth and competitive positioning.

**Business Objective :**

* The primary objective of this analysis is to:
* Evaluate restaurant performance based on ratings and customer engagement
* Analyze pricing distribution and its impact on ratings
* Identify high-demand cuisines
* Assess geographic concentration of restaurants
* Examine the influence of online ordering and table booking features

**Dataset Overview :**

Source File: Zomato data.csv
The dataset includes the following key attributes:
* Restaurant Name
* Location
* Cuisines Offered
* Aggregate Ratings
* Votes (Customer Engagement)
* Cost for Two
* Online Order Availability
* Table Booking Availability

**Methodology :**

1. Data Preparation:
* Missing value treatment
* Duplicate record removal
* Data type standardization
* Feature cleaning and formatting

2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):
* Rating distribution analysis
* Price range segmentation
* Location-wise restaurant density analysis
* Cuisine popularity evaluation
* Feature impact analysis (Online Order & Table Booking)

3. Visualization & Insight Generation:
* Bar charts for categorical comparison
* Distribution plots for rating and cost analysis
* Correlation analysis for engagement metrics

**Key Business Insights :**
* Restaurants offering online ordering demonstrate higher customer engagement levels.
* A limited set of cuisines dominates overall market demand.
* Mid-range pricing categories tend to receive stronger customer ratings.
* Certain locations show high market saturation, indicating intense competition.

**Strategic Recommendations :**
* Strengthen digital ordering capabilities to improve engagement and retention.
* Optimize pricing strategy within competitive mid-range segments.
* Target high-density restaurant zones with differentiated marketing strategies.
* Focus on quality improvement initiatives to enhance customer ratings.
